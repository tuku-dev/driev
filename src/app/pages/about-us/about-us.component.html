driEV is an EV bike sharing company based in Bhubaneswar with a vision of making urban commute affordable and sustainable. We are the 1st EV bike sharing start-up in eastern India providing smart mobility options with zero tailpipe emissions. Born at the peak of the pandemic, we have kept scaling new heights throughout the tough times.driEV bikes are embedded with latest technologies to provide the greatest user experience by monitoring the various parameters of the bike like, speed, mileage, location and battery percentage, so the customers just cruise along. driEV also provides micro services such as helmets,zero insurance hassles and No Maintenance worries on renting EVs and anything that provides a better user experience. Our vision is to make urban commute accessible, affordable and sustainable, what is currently a start-up is prepped to scale to make ownership of two-wheelers redundant by being just a click away for customers, no matter how unique their demands are.
