How To Charge?

There are two ways to charge your vehicle. You can use the convenience of swappable Battery (If you have that model) to take out your battery and charge in the comforts of your home. Steps to remember:

  • Switch off the MCB first to avoid electric shock
  • Take out the battery after removing the strap
  • And Charge the battery after using your connector

or

Just plug in the charger and find the socket just beneath the front of your seat and charge.

NOTE - Do not charge the battery overnight Avoid deep discharge

Where To Charge?

Find a 5 amp socket to plug in your charger. In order to recognise a 5Amp socket, identify the 3 pin socket where you would normally charge your laptop.

What is the range?

It varies from model to model and rider to rider. Riding behaviours govern mileage to a lot of extent but do reach out to our Customer Support to know the true range of your vehicle.

How long does it take to full Charge?

It normally takes 3-3.5 hours to take a battery from 0% to 100%.
